An intelligent navigation platform that prioritizes traveler security. By combining statistical accident hot-spots with real-time routing algorithms, NaviSafe provides travelers with optimized routes focused on safety alongside traditional speed.

  • Developed an AI-powered safety-first navigation platform integrating accident-prone area analysis with real-time routing.
